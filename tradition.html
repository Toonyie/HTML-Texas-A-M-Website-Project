Bonfire Memorial

Aggie Bonfire Memorial is a tradition at Texas A&M University. The tradition occurs on November 18th every year and is meant to commemorate those who have been involved with the bonfire collapse accident on November 18, 1999. It is a symbol of Aggie spirit, unity, and tradition. Tours are able to be requested for the Bonfire Memorial located at the Texas A&M traditions website where you can learn about the history, events, and tradition of the Bonfire Memorial.

When and Where Does the Tradition Takes Place?
  1. When: November 18, 2:42 a.m
  2. Location: Texas A&M University Bonfire Memorial

Who is Involved in the Creation of the Tradition?
  1. Texas A&M University
  2. Texas A&M University Students and Former Students (Aggies)
