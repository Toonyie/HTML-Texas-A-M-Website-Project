Philip Tran

Philip Tran is a Computer Engineering Student at Texas A&M University. Born on January 29, 2004 in Houston Texas, he had an incredibly difficult time in knowing what his goals and aspirations are. He decided to study at Texas A&M University's engineering program as he saw that he has a niche for engineering. 3 years in, he has found his passion and found an interest in a business minor to further broaden his horizons. Currently, he is taking a business minor class to learn the fundamentals of business as well as an extracurricular course that gives him on hand experience of digital marketing. He enjoys learning new ideas and concepts and is willing to take the time to have a solid understanding of the material. He enjoys playing video games with friends and enjoys action games the most.
