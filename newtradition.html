Aggineers Assemble

Terminology

Aggineers
An upcoming or freshman Aggie Engineer
AgGear Up
A program designed to prepare upcoming freshman engineering aggies with tools, knowledge, and advice to help prepare them in their first year at A&M
ETAM Launchpad
A program designed to be the "launching point" for engineering studnets to be successful of going through the ETAM process and get them prepared for their journey ahead

Aggineers Assemble is a newfound tradition at Texas A&M University. It is the tradition to unite all upcoming Aggie freshmen who are interested in the engineering program Texas A&M prides itself on. Taking inspiration from other freshman traditions such as Fish Camp, upcoming Aggineers will feel welcomed to the campus, and understand the Aggie spirit while also preparing themselves for a successful first year. The tradition will not only introduce Aggie culture, but also provide information for Aggineers to learn about Engineering processes such as ETAM and some friendly advice that makes the ETAM process less intimidating.
